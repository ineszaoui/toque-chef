TOQUE CHEF

A card game where you have to seduce a jury of chefs with pastries!

Try out the game online

Theme: Pastry

You are a pastry chef in a small village. You hear about a pastry competition, with the golden hat as the prize. You pass all the stages of the competition with flying colours, there is only one competitor left to beat. In order to win, win the win, win the jury's favour by serving them their favourite pastry. Look for hidden clues to find out what each judge likes best.

Quick rules of the game

Number of players : 2

Principle: Offer the most pastries (cards) to a jury at the end of a round to gain their favour.

Victory :

Convince at least 4 juries

Obtain 11 victory points

Have the most VPs at the end of the 3 rounds
